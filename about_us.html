About Us

Our goal is to promote vegetarianism by familiarizing people with rare known benefits of vegetarianism along with the health risks of meat eating all for the sake of animal rights. Major reasons for people not being able to support the animal right ideas and abstain from meat eating are health factors and question such as 'Where would I get my proteins from?' or 'Does vegetarian food only consist of salads?' All such questions shall be answered here in an effort to promote animal rights movement.

'Animal Rights' is one of the major reasons for people of various age groups to repulse meat. Animal rights activists mostly have hunting, harvesting, factory farming, experimentation-testing of animals on their list but 'Slaughtering Animals'

Our mission is to make people aware of benefits of vegetarianism. So that their transition from a meat-eater to vegetarian becomes smoother and anxiety-free.
