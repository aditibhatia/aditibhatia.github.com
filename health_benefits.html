Health Risks of Meat-Eating!

Vegetarians are protected from various health risks because of their diet practice, that meat eaters often have to face.

Large scale studies have shown vegetarianism to significantly lower the risks of heart diseases, the fat and cholesterol in meat boost the amount of cholesterol in consumers' blood, which leads to heart attacks.

Cancer, obesity, hypertension, high blood pressure and diabetes are also more common amongst meat-eaters than vegetarians. Also, mercury traces in fish, mad cow disease, bird flu and swine flu, are the nightmares for meat-eaters which could apparently attack anyone of them anytime like a monster, whether unheard of before or known.

Additionally, the women who eat meat on a daily basis are almost 4 times more likely to get breast cancer than those women who eat little or no meat. Besides all, vegetarian diet is hormone-free.
