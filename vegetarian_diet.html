What does Vegetarian Diet Include?

Food can be found everywhere, from the golden arches to the scaly fins in the sea but have you every wondered what the vegetarians eat? Fruits, vegetables, nuts, grains, beans and milk products are part of a diet that to some of us might seem strange.

Sans-Meat

Vegetarian diet consist of almost all the food products that abstains from animal killing. Vegetarianism is practice of a diet that excludes all the animal flesh and sea food. Vegetarians have to follow dietary restrictions. The diet excludes the skin of any living being.

Essential Nutrients in Vegetarian Diet?

Vegetarian diet along with dairy products provide more than sufficient proteins if taken from variety of sources. For vegetarians there are many alternative protein sources instead of meat. In nature soybean is considered to be the highest source of protein which is roughly three times more than meat. Tofu is one of the most common forms of soybean. Lot of pulse, lentils, chickpeas, green peas and nuts also contain double the amount of protein as compared to the meat. The staple diet for Vegetarians is wheat, corn and rice.
